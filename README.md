# 🌍 Afripay Finance 🚀  

### **Seamless Cross-Border Payments for West & Central Africa, Built on Stellar**  

![Afripay Finance Overview](https://your-image-link.com/afripay-overview.png)  

## 🏆 **What is Afripay Finance?**  
Afripay Finance is a **blockchain-based payment infrastructure** that simplifies **cross-border remittances, business transactions, and stablecoin management** in West & Central Africa. We use the **Stellar blockchain** to enable:  

✅ **Instant remittances** for individuals & businesses.  
✅ **Fiat on/off-ramps** for XAF & XOF stablecoins.  
✅ **Soroban smart contracts** for automation & compliance.  
✅ **Secure, low-cost transactions** using blockchain.  

---

## 🌟 **How It Works**  

### **1️⃣ Asset Issuance & Management**  
Afripay issues **XAF & XOF stablecoins** on Stellar, pegged 1:1 to local fiat currencies.  
- **XAF (Central African CFA Franc)**
- **XOF (West African CFA Franc)**  

💰 **Use Cases**:  
- Send & receive payments **instantly** across borders.  
- Convert stablecoins to **fiat (CFA)** using local partners.  
- Pay for goods & services via **Afripay-integrated wallets**.  

📌 **Stablecoin Flow:**  
![Stablecoin Flow](https://your-image-link.com/stablecoin-flow.png)  

---

### **2️⃣ Deposits & Withdrawals (On/Off-Ramps)**
Afripay integrates **SEP-6, SEP-24 & SEP-31** to enable fiat conversions.  

🔹 **Deposit** CFA → Receive XAF/XOF tokens.  
🔹 **Withdraw** XAF/XOF → Receive CFA via **Mobile Money, Bank Transfer**.  

📌 **On/Off-Ramp Process:**  
![On-Off Ramp](https://your-image-link.com/onoff-ramp.png)  

🌍 **Current Supported Corridors:**  
✔ **Cameroon 🇨🇲 (XAF) → Nigeria 🇳🇬 (NGNT)**  
✔ **XAF/XOF Liquidity via Cowrie & Kado**  

---

### **3️⃣ Soroban Smart Contracts**
Afripay utilizes **Soroban smart contracts** for:  
🔄 **Automated Stablecoin Management** (XAF/XOF issuance & redemption).  
⚡ **Recurring Payments** (Subscription-based transactions).  
💼 **Business Payments** (Escrow & smart invoicing).
